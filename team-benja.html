Chief Operating Officer (COO)

Benjamin Paul is the Chief Operating Officer of the Technical Department at St. East Africa Renewable Solution Limited. He also serves as a Senior Inspector in the Department of Energy and Electrification at Machakos County Government, Kenya.

With over 18 years of experience in electrical and solar installations, Benjamin has overseen major renewable energy projects and infrastructure implementations. His leadership combines deep technical expertise with hands-on experience in energy system design, inspection, and safety compliance.

As COO, he drives operational excellence, oversees installation quality assurance, and ensures that Solartyme’s renewable energy solutions meet both international standards and local energy demands.

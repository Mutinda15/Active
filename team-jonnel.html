Chief Executive Officer

Jonnell Carol Minefee is the CEO of Solartyme USA LLC. She is also the Chairwoman of the Georgia Solar Energy Association and Vice President of the US-Africa Trade Commission.

With her leadership and vision, Jonnell drives the company’s mission of advancing clean, sustainable energy solutions across the U.S. and Africa.
